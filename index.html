PROJECT 1
FRC
2024
The robot we built in 2024, named “toothless”, features a tank drivetrain, an arm, a dual-purpose shooter and intake, and a climber. As a programmer I worked on creating different subsystems and integrating them all to make the driving and autonomous as smooth as possible. Something new we did this year was leveraging a vision model, triangulating the position of the robot using multiple April tags and adjusting the arm height to shoot the pieces at the correct trajectory according to our robot’s distance from the goal.
PROJECT 2
FRC
2023
The robot we built in 2023, named “fireduck” features a tank drivetrain, a telescoping arm, meaning it can rotate and extend, and a shooter/intake designed to pick up both cones and cubes. The programming utilized PDI feedback loops and arm feedforward to ensure all movements ran smoothly.
PROJECT 3
BOTANICAL BESTIE
2023
Made for HackTheValley 8, our project, dubbed “botanical bestie” was a flower pot meant to bring your plants to life. Based on readings from various sensors such as soil moisture, humidity, and light, our plant would talk to its owner and ask to be cared for when its stats were low! The pot featured an LCD and 8x8 LED matrix that displayed its stats and a camera to know when people were near!
